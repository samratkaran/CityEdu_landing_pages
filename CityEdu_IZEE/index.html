About IZee

IZee has a history that dates back to 2004, when a top-notch recruitment organization called “IZee Manpower” was established by IIM Alumni.

In 2009, the college came into existence, with a clear mission of becoming a globally connected Top B Schools in Bangalore. Its vision is to provide transformative research and teaching that positions the college at the forefront of influential knowledge and leadership.

Our IZee campus with World Class Facility, situated in Bangalore, offers various courses including BBA, BBA Aviation, BCA, B.Com and MBA with a strategic advantage to capitalize on the numerous prospects presented by this dynamic city. It is a top-notch facility that provides a world-class learning environment.
